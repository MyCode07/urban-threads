HOW TO MEASURE

CHEST

Measure around the widest part of your chest.

WAIST

Take the measurement across the waistband of your most well-fitting trousers and multiply it by two to determine your waist circumference. Alternatively, measure around your waist at the desired pants position (typically near the top of your hipbones).
